About

My name is Warren. I am a student at St. John's School, and I designed this website for our Captstone Project in my eighth grade Civics class. I enjoy biking, and I want other people in Houston to bike also. I made this website in order to spread word about some of the good bike trails in Houston. I also enjoy web development, so I wrote this website from scratch. I am still improving it, so it is a little rough around the edges.
