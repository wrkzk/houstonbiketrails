Memorial Park Orange Trail

Map

Video

Description

I would consider this to be one of the hardes trails at Memorial Park. This trail branches off of the Purple Trail at about one third of the way through of the Purple Trail. This trail starts and ends with a board walk. The one at the beginning is a short little bridge over a ravine, and the one at the end is considerably steeper (but still fun). There are many roots on this trail. Also, watch out for other bikers, because this is one of the most popular Memorial Park biking trails.
Difficulty level: moderate to hard.
