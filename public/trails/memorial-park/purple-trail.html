Memorial Park Purple Trail

Map

Video

Description

This Purple Trail serves as an acess trail for the other trails, as well as being a bike trail in its own right. This trail starts from the parking lot at the end of North Picnic Lane. The first third of this trail is easy, and fairly flat, with a bit of downhill. About a third of the way through, the trail becomes barely bikable, with steep, and sandy slopes. The second third of this trail is also fairly flat, with really dense brush on all sides. Watch out for various muddy spots in this area too. The last third of the trail parallels the railroad tracks, and is wide with plenty of space. Watch for walkers and dogs on this trail.
Difficulty level: easy.
