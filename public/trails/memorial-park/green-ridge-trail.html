Memorial Park Green Ridge Trail

Map

Video

Description

Starting from South Picnic Lane, this trail cuts through a large grassy feild before entering the more forested parts of Memorial Park. The first half of the trail is more technical, with a rooted ravine at the beginning. After the technical section, this trail starts to flow more. You begin to swoop in and out of small ravines. Watch out, because there are lots of blind turns where you could easily collide with a someone.
Difficulty level: moderate.
