Memorial Park Yellow Trail

Map

Video

Description

This trail starts off next to the head of red trail. It starts of easy, but gets harder toward the middle and end. In the middle, this trail also starts to get more technical, with a few roots. This trail has some very sharp turns, so watch out. towards the end, make sure to stay right, otherwise you will branch off the trail, and merge with another trail. This trail hooks up with the purple trail.
Difficulty level: Moderate.
