Anthills Part 3

Map

Video

Description

This is my least favorite section of the anthills trail, even though I still love it. This section is mostly flat, and stays up and far away from the bayou, as you can see on the map. This section is also very flat, and where I encountered the most people (as you can see in the video). If you have to choose which sections to do because of time limitations, you should pick this one last.
Difficulty level: easy to moderate.
