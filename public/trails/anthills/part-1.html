Anthills Part 1

Map

Video

Description

In my opinion, the first part of this trail is the most fun. It starts by the side of the road at Eldridge, and continues down by the bayou. Watch out at the start, because recently somebody put a fence in, so be prepared to go a short ways around. This trail follows the bayou and is fairly technical. Also, if there is a split in the trails, don't be alarmed. Anthills has many side trails that diverge and come back to the main trail.
Difficulty level: Moderate.
