Anthills Part 2

Map

Video

Description

Watch out for the section of fence that prevents you from going right into this section from the previous one. This section of the trail goes from up above the bayou, to right down next to the bayou. Be careful that you fall in. Also, if there had been recent rain or flooding, I would not recommend this section of the trail, due to it being so close to the side of the bayou. This section has some areas where you might need to dismount, due to drainage pipes.
Difficulty level: moderate.
